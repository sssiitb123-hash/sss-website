What We Make

One material.
Endless applications.

Our mycelium composite replaces plastic, foam, leather, and timber across five product categories — all grown from Parali.

🪑

Furniture

Tables, chairs, panels, and modular systems — grown to any geometry, as strong as conventional materials, and fully compostable at end of life.

📦

Packaging

Custom-moulded protective packaging that replaces EPS foam. Biodegrades in 45 days at home — no microplastics, no landfill.

🔇

Sound Isolation Panels

Mycelium's naturally porous structure delivers excellent acoustic absorption. Sustainable panels for studios, offices, and hospitality spaces.

🌿

Interior Décor

Sculptural wall pieces, partition screens, and decorative elements that bring living texture into commercial and residential interiors.

🧵

Textile Alternatives

Leather and upholstery substitutes grown from mycelium — soft, durable, and animal-free. A direct drop-in for conventional leather in furniture and fashion.
